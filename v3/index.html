PivotMaster V0.0.3

Moves: 0

The third version that introduced the ghost square preview.
Now, you can see where the square will move before you make the move.
This makes it a bit easier to plan your moves and avoid mistakes.
The rotation logic is fixed, and the ghost square shows the future position of the square.
The basic game mechanics are in place, but there's still room for layout and scoring
as well as visual and UX improvements.
